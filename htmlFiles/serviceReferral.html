Service Referral

Regional Center referrals can be made by submitting a Purchase of Service (POS) to Future Transitions, Inc. along with a completed referral form either online or via fax. Please include a current IEP and any other report which might contain pertinent information regarding the consumer's needs.

Fax: (661) 723 - 0217

*Proudly serving developmentally disabled persons in CVRC, ELARC, KRC, LRC, NLACRC, SCLARC, and SG/PRC!
