Parent Training Program

Parenting Vendor: PL0706

The Parent Training Program teaches independence by providing intensive, one-to-one parent training to developmentally disabled parents.

Program Highlights:

Child Safety

Bonding with your children

We cover the following areas:

  • Bonding with your children
  • Identifying and addressing the Parenting Problem
  • Child Development Prenatal - 18 years
  • Child Safety
  • Nutrition
  • Child Abuse Prevention

We cover the following areas:

  • Discipline and Guidance
  • Communication
  • Building Self-Esteem
  • Dealing with stress
  • Developing a structured routine
  • Developing a Peaceful and Harmonious Living Environment

*Proudly serving developmentally disabled persons in CVRC, ELARC, KRC, LRC, NLACRC, SCLARC, and SG/PRC!
